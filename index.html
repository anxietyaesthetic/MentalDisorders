About Me

Hi! My name is Jeriel, a Puerto Rican Master's student in Network Security at the InterAmerican University of Puerto Rico, Guayama Campus. I’m also a mental health patient who has lived with a variety of disorders most of my life. Through therapy and the appropriate medication, I’ve learned to live with them and continue growing every day.

My Diagnosis of Disorders

  • Anxiety
  • Paranoid Schizophrenia
  • Social Anxiety

My Personal Symptoms

Anxiety: Irritability, muscle tension, heart palpitations, shortness of breath, stomach problems, and nausea.

Paranoid Schizophrenia: Delusions, hallucinations, social withdrawal, lack of motivation, and reduced emotional expression.

Social Anxiety: Fear of judgment, self-consciousness, excessive worry about actions, and difficulty engaging in social interactions.
