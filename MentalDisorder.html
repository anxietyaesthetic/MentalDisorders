About Me!

Hi! My name is Jeriel, a Puerto Rican, who is a Master's Network Security Student at InterAmerican University of Puerto Rico, Guayama Campus. Also, I am a Mental-Patient who has lived with a variety of disorders most of my life. But I learned to live with them thanks to therapy and the appropiate medication.

My Diagnosis of Disorders

My Diagnosis up to now are:

    ~Anxiety
    ~Paranoid Schizophrenia
    ~Socal Anxiety

My Personal Symptoms of Each Disorders

For the Anxiety Disorder, I can be irritable, experience muscle tensions, heart palpitations, be short of breath, stomach problems & nauseas.

For the Paranoid Schizophrenia, I can experience delusions, and at the same time hallucinations. And in worst cases, I can avoid friends and family, lose motivation to socialize and, most of the time, reduce emotional expression.

And for Social Anxiety, I always feared of being judged, be self-concerned, worry about every action I make, I avoid a lot of social interaction, and have a hard time interacting.
